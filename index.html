Background

I am an alumni of Bucknell University who majored in Computer Science and Engineering in the Class of 2018! As a first generation college student pursuing the American Dream, I am passionate about using my education to give empower my community and my family to create impact in the world. I am currently a software engineer at Microsoft located in Redmond, Washington!

Home and Hobbies

Calgary, Canada is my birth place but Boston will always be my home. I immigrated from Canada to Boston when I was 6 and lived here ever since. Growing up, I enjoyed activities such as Yu-Gi-Oh, basketball, break-dancing, tennis, gymnastics, football, and so much more. It was during my 10th grade at my high school when I took my first computer science course and fell in love with subject ever since.

Dreams

I grew up in the projects and lived in a low-income household. My parents had 4 children to take care of, with only my dad providing for the family. My dad worked at Target. They are immigrants who moved from their homeland with 0 knowledge of english nor the cultural customs of America. I was ignorant as a child to the situation my family was in and how hard my parents were fighting to provide their children opportunities. One of my main goals in life is to move my family out of the projects and back into an actual house. I want to give them the life they never had. Other goals I strive to reach include learning as much as I can from companies I will be working for and using everything I learned to eventually start my own company!
