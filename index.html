Background

As a graduate of Bucknell University with a major in Computer Science and Engineering, I am proud to be one of the first in my family to attend college and strive towards achieving the American Dream. I am driven by my passion to leverage my education in empowering my community and family to make a positive impact on the world.

Home and Hobbies

I was born in Calgary, Canada, but Boston has always felt like home to me. My family immigrated to Boston when I was six years old, and I have been living here ever since. During my childhood, I was always engaged in various activities such as basketball, break-dancing, tennis, gymnastics, football, snowboarding, and even playing Yu-Gi-Oh. However, it wasn't until I took my first computer science course during my sophomore year of high school that I discovered my passion for the subject, which has continued to grow ever since.

Dreams

Growing up in the Newland Projects in Boston, I saw firsthand the challenges that families face when resources are limited. My parents worked tirelessly to provide for our family of six, with my dad working at Target to make ends meet. As immigrants who arrived in the US without any knowledge of English or American customs, they faced additional hurdles. However, their determination and resilience taught me the value of hard work and perseverance. Because of my upbringing, I'm committed to giving back to my community and investing in projects that empower and enable individuals.
